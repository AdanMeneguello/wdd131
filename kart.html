What is karting?

Karting is the gateway to motorsport. Short “arrive & drive” sessions in rental karts — no license required.

  • 10–25 minute sessions
  • Equipment included (suit & helmet)
  • Timing and lap charts

How to get started

  1. Pick a track and check pricing/availability.
  2. Book online.
  3. Arrive 20 minutes early for briefing and gear up.

About Karting

  1. Karting is a motorsport that serves as both a recreational activity and a training ground for professional racing.
  2. It originated in the late 1950s in the United States.
  3. The first kart was built in 1956 by Art Ingels in California.

Driving tips

  • Look far ahead, not at the bumper in front of you.
  • Brake in a straight line, release before turning.
  • Racing line: outside → apex → outside.
  • Be smooth on the wheel; avoid locking the tires.

Safety

  • Full‑face helmet and visor down.
  • Hair tucked in; nothing loose on clothing.
  • Obey flags and the instructor’s briefing.
